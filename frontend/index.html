Dashboard

BoxMini live control surface

Matrix

Wheel zooms, hovering near an edge pans the cartridge, Middle drag or CtrlDrag pans manually, Drag box-selects droplets, and Double click resets the view.

Selected droplets show a translucent target preview over empty electrodes; R rotates the group and Click commits a SIPP move from Live view.

Right click queues a single selected droplet target; ShiftRight click appends another queued waypoint.

Painting

Paint opens electrode tools; drag rectangles to mark blocked electrodes, activate electrodes, or erase electrodes and affected droplet shape.

Stage And Droplets

ShiftClick moves the stage to the electrode under the cursor; Arrow keys nudge the selected droplet shape.

Timeline

Wheel zooms the visible time range around the cursor; ShiftWheel pans time.

Drag scrubs preview time; use the range bar handles to choose exactly which time window is shown.

Plan Executor contains real matrix execution controls; Trim Tail deletes planned frames after the selected frame.

Streamer

Wheel zooms the camera stream, Drag or Middle drag pans while zoomed, and Double click resets the camera view.

Conversation

CtrlAltN creates and joins a new run; Set uses the prompt box as the active goal.

Send starts the agent, Steer redirects it while busy, and Queue adds the prompt after the current turn.
